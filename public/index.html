What's CONFIDING?

We all have secrets and desire to share. COFIDING is a platform let you share the stories to people physically around you in limited time. It's a outlet of your secret, emotion and experience with anonymous listener around you. Your post will be removed after a day to let your secret remain secret.


CONFIDING helps you reduce pressure by sharing ephemeral content to a group of people, because it limits the amount of damage an ill-thought-out post can do. Also, limited distance gives a live feed of what's going on around you.


You need to create a new post to see the posts around you.

Code of conduct

Cyberbully and revealing privacy information are NOT allowed in CONFIDING. Any of the following behavior is unacceptable:

  • Offensive comments
  • Threats of violence
  • Stalking or following
  • Illegal purposes
  • Any kind of harassment

Please make sure you're posting quality content and keep this place clean together. If you believe someone is violating the code of conduct, we ask that you report it by emailing tyh289@nyu.edu. Please include your name and a description of the incident, and we will get back to you ASAP.
