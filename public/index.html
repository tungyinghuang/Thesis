What's BEANS?

Spill the beans here! BEANS is a platform letting you share the stories to people physically around you in limited time. It's a outlet of your secret, emotion and experience with anonymous listener around you. Every post is a bean, and it will:

  • Remain on the page for 24 hours
  • Be seen by others who are less 8 km away from you
  • Be a ticket. Create a new bean to see the beans around you

BEANS wants to create a hidden place that makes you feel comfortable pouring out your emotion and stories. You don't know who's the listeners and nobody knows you're here. Just leave your story and let the time take it away. You'll feel better if you get the matter off your chest.

Code of conduct

Cyberbully and revealing privacy information are NOT allowed in BEANS. Any of the following behavior is unacceptable:

  • Offensive comments
  • Threats of violence
  • Stalking or following
  • Illegal purposes
  • Any kind of harassment

Please make sure you're posting quality content and keep this place clean together. If you believe someone is violating the code of conduct, we ask that you report it by emailing tyh289@nyu.edu. Please include your name and a description of the incident, and we will get back to you ASAP.
